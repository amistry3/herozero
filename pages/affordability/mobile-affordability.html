How This Calculator Works on 10-2-6-20 Rule

The 10% Rule

The price of the phone should not exceed 10% of your annual salary. This ensures that the purchase doesn't strain your finances.

The 2-Year Usage Rule

You should plan to use the phone for at least two years. This helps in maximizing the value of the device and reduces the frequency of upgrades.

The 6-Month EMI Rule

If you are planning to buy the phone on EMI, the tenure should not be more than 6 months. This prevents you from being locked into a long-term financial commitment for a depreciating asset.

The 20% Upfront Rule

You should be able to pay at least 20% of the phone's price upfront. This reduces the loan amount and the interest burden.
