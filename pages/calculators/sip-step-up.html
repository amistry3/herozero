SIP Step-up Calculator

Model monthly SIPs where the SIP amount increases by a fixed percentage each year. Useful to see how disciplined step-ups accelerate corpus growth.

Assumes monthly SIP and annual step-up at year boundary.
How to use

Enter your current monthly SIP and expected annual return. The Annual step-up is applied once every 12 months to the monthly SIP amount (for example, 10% step-up increases the SIP from ₹5,000 to ₹5,500 in year 2).
