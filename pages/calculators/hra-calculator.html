HRA rules

HRA exemption uses the minimum of: actual HRA received, rent paid − 10% of basic, or 50%/40% of basic depending on city.

Estimate note

This is an estimator and rounds values; consult HR/tax advisor for exact calculations and applicability.

FAQ
What if rent ≤ 10% basic?
Then rent − 10% basic becomes zero or negative and does not increase exemption; exemption will be lower.
