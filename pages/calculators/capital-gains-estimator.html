About Capital Gains (simple)

When you sell an investment for more than you paid, the extra amount is a capital gain. Taxes on that gain depend on how long you held the asset.

Holding periods

  • Short-term: Sold within 365 days — treated as short-term and taxed at the STCG rate.
  • Long-term: Held more than 365 days — treated as long-term and taxed at the LTCG rate (some exemptions may apply).

Important notes

  • This calculator gives a simple estimate. Actual tax depends on detailed rules, indexation, fund type, and personal tax brackets.
  • As of 2025 this estimator uses STCG = 20% and LTCG = 12.5% with a ₹1,00,000 LTCG exemption for equity mutual funds (simplified rules).
  • For precise tax calculations, consult a tax professional or the official tax guidance.
