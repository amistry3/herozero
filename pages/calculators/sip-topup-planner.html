SIP Top-up Planner

Plan periodic SIP increases (top-ups) and see final value.

Why plan SIP top-ups?

Increasing your SIP periodically (top-up) helps your savings keep pace with inflation and income growth. Even modest annual top-ups compound significantly over long periods and can boost your final corpus.

How this planner works

  1. We assume you invest the monthly SIP each month for the year and it grows at the annual rate you enter (compounded monthly for contributions).
  2. At the end of each year the SIP amount increases by the annual top-up percentage you specify.
  3. The calculator shows the final maturity with your top-up plan and compares it to keeping the SIP constant (no top-ups).

How to use

  • Enter your current monthly SIP and a realistic top-up percentage (for example 5–15%).
  • Choose an expected annual return — this is an estimate, not a guarantee.
  • Click Calculate to see final values and a year-by-year growth chart comparing with and without top-ups.
