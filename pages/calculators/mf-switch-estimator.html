About this MF Switch / Exit Estimator

Use this tool when you are considering switching between mutual fund schemes or exiting a fund holding. It helps estimate the holding period, gain, and approximate tax payable on the gain so you can compare the tax impact of switching now versus later.

Why this matters

  • Switching between funds is treated as a sale for tax purposes — gains may attract capital gains tax even if you stay within the same fund house.
  • Knowing the holding period and estimated tax helps you decide whether to switch immediately, wait to qualify for long-term treatment, or adjust your exit amount.
  • The tool provides a quick, conservative estimate to guide your decision; for exact tax filing figures consult your tax advisor or fund statements.

Tax rules used

This estimator uses the following simplified assumptions for quick comparison:

  • Long-term capital gains (LTCG): 12.5% (after applicable exemptions)
  • Short-term capital gains (STCG): 20%
