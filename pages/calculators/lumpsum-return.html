Understanding Lumpsum Returns

A lumpsum investment means putting a single amount of money into an investment at one time. This calculator helps you see how much your money can grow over time with annual compounding.

How it works

When you invest a lumpsum, your money earns returns each year. These returns get added to your investment, so next year you earn returns on a bigger amount. This is called compounding, and it helps your money grow faster over time.

Why use lumpsum investing?

  • Good if you have a large amount ready to invest.
  • Simple to manage since you invest once.
  • Can grow significantly if invested for a long time.

Things to keep in mind

  • Returns depend on market performance and are not guaranteed.
  • Investing early and staying invested longer usually helps grow your money more.
  • You can combine lumpsum investing with regular investments like SIPs for a balanced approach.
