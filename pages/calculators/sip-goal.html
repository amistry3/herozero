About this SIP Goal Planner

This tool estimates the monthly SIP you need to reach a future amount. Enter your target amount, the number of years you can invest, and an expected annual return. The planner shows the monthly amount to save and a year-by-year projection.

Simple steps

  1. Decide how much money you want in the future (your goal).
  2. Choose how many years you can keep investing.
  3. Pick a reasonable expected annual return (this is only an estimate).
  4. Click Calculate — the planner shows the monthly SIP required and a projection.

Things to remember

  • The result is an estimate. Actual returns depend on market moves and fund costs.
  • Higher expected returns lower the required monthly SIP, but higher returns usually mean higher risk.
  • You can adjust the SIP later — many funds let you increase, decrease, pause, or stop contributions.

Quick FAQ

Minimum monthly amount: Depends on the fund; many funds accept small SIPs (e.g., ₹500/month).

How long should I invest? For goals affected by market ups and downs (like retirement or wealth creation), longer periods (several years) help smooth returns.
