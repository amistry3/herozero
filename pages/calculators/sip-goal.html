Formula Inversion

Monthly SIP ≈ Goal × r_m / (((1+r_m)^n - 1) × (1+r_m)) where r_m = annual% / 12 and n = months.

Inflation Angle

Adjust nominal target upward if goal is far out (e.g. 10y+). Real purchasing power declined otherwise.
