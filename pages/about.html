About HeroZero

We build simple, reliable tools and guides to help people in India make better financial decisions. Our calculators and articles are designed to be practical, easy to use and transparent about assumptions.

Our mission

To make useful financial tools accessible to everyone. We focus on clear explanations and calculators that people can use right away to plan purchases, savings and investments.

Trust & transparency

We monetise the site to keep it free and maintained. Our revenue sources include display advertising, affiliate links and occasional sponsored content. We clearly label sponsored articles and affiliate links and never accept payments that would influence honest recommendations.

The team

A small team of makers and finance enthusiasts maintain the site. For media or partnership enquiries, please use the Contact page.

How we maintain accuracy

  • We use standard formulas and realistic assumptions for calculators.
  • Guides are regularly reviewed and updated when rules (taxes, rates) change.
  • We welcome corrections — contact us if you spot issues.
