About these calculators

Each tool gives a quick, realistic estimate using standard formulas and sensible defaults. They help you compare scenarios, understand which inputs matter and plan next steps. Use results for education and decision support — pair with professional advice for high‑stakes financial commitments.

Car Affordability

Estimates EMI, indicative fuel & ownership cost from on‑road price, down payment, rate and usage (km/month) so you keep EMIs within a safe share of income.

House Affordability

Shows mortgage EMI and impact of altering tenure or down payment, helping you judge comfort vs. stretch on housing decisions.

Mobile Affordability

Quick EMI vs. lump‑sum check to avoid crowding other monthly priorities when upgrading devices.

Stock Average Price

Computes weighted average buy price across lots so you track breakeven while averaging in.

SIP Calculator

Projects future value of monthly Systematic Investment Plans under assumed annual returns.

SIP Goal Planner

Back‑solves required monthly SIP to reach a target corpus by a deadline.

Lumpsum Return

Illustrates growth of a one‑time investment with annual compounding for horizon planning.

SIP vs Lumpsum

Compares outcomes investing the same capital via periodic SIP or immediate lumpsum.

XIRR (cashflows)

Calculates internal rate of return for irregular dated contributions and withdrawals.

Capital Gains Estimator

Rough STCG / LTCG estimate (India) including basic equity LTCG exemption awareness.

Portfolio Rebalancer

Suggests buy / sell amounts to restore target asset allocation after market moves.

Expense Ratio Impact

Shows long‑run drag of higher fees so you quantify savings of lower expense ratio options.

SIP Top-up Planner

Models scheduled annual SIP increases (top‑ups) and their acceleration effect.

SIP Step-up

Applies a fixed annual percentage raise to your monthly SIP to visualise compounding of contributions.

SWP / Withdrawal

Estimates corpus durability under systematic withdrawals in retirement or drawdown.

MF Switch / Exit

Approximates tax impact when switching or exiting mutual fund holdings.

MF Direct vs Regular

Compares corpus difference from direct vs regular plan expense ratios for both SIP & lumpsum.

Assumptions & accuracy

Formulas use simplifying assumptions and ignore future tax / fee changes. Treat outputs as educational estimates — validate critical decisions with a qualified professional.
