About these calculators

Each calculator on this page is designed to give a quick, realistic estimate to help you make better financial decisions. They use standard formulas and reasonable assumptions — see the short summaries below to understand what inputs matter and how to interpret the results.

Car Affordability

Estimates EMI, lifetime fuel cost and total ownership cost using the on-road price, down payment, interest rate and typical usage (monthly km). Useful to compare models and choose a comfortable EMI percentage of your monthly net income.

House Affordability

Calculates monthly mortgage/EMI for given loan amount, tenure and rate. Helps you gauge whether a property fits your budget and what effect changing down payment or tenure has on monthly cashflow.

Mobile Affordability

Quick check to decide if a mobile purchase (outright or EMI) is affordable given your monthly disposable income and priorities.

Stock Average Price

Computes the weighted average purchase price across multiple buy orders. Useful when averaging into positions and tracking breakeven price.

SIP Calculator

Estimates future value of regular monthly SIPs using an expected annual return. Useful for goal planning and comparing SIP durations.

SIP Goal Planner

Given a future goal and expected returns, calculates the monthly SIP required to reach that goal within the chosen timeframe.

Lumpsum Return

Projects the growth of a one-time investment under annual compounding — useful for retirement lumpsum and inheritance planning.

SIP vs Lumpsum

Compares final values when investing the same total either as a SIP or as a lump-sum to help decide which approach fits market conditions and your cashflow.

XIRR (cashflows)

Computes the internal rate of return for dated cash flows (investments and redemptions). Good for measuring portfolio performance across irregular contributions.

Capital Gains Estimator

Simple estimator for short-term and long-term capital gains on equity and mutual fund sales in India. Includes a basic LTCG ₹1L exemption rule for equity funds.

Portfolio Rebalancer

Given current holdings and target allocations, shows how much to buy or sell in each asset to restore the desired allocation.

Expense Ratio Impact

Compares end values after accounting for different expense ratios, highlighting the long-term cost of higher fees.

SIP Top-up Planner

Models periodic SIP increases (top-ups) and shows how annual increases accelerate corpus growth over time.

SWP / Withdrawal Planner

Estimates how long a retirement corpus will last given a fixed periodic withdrawal and expected return on remaining corpus.

MF Switch / Exit Estimator

Helps estimate tax outcomes when switching between mutual funds or exiting a holding; treat as an estimator only and consult tax advice for complex cases.

Assumptions & accuracy

These calculators provide estimates based on the inputs you provide and reasonable default formulas. They do not account for every possible fee, tax change or insurance variation. Use results as guidance and consult a professional for high-stakes decisions.
